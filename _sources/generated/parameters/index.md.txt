# 📦 Applications

| Application name | Features | Parameters |
|---|---|---|
| `mp-advec-diff` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>transient scalar advection-diffusion transport</li><li>prescribed velocity fields</li><li>CG-FEM (using SUPG stabilization)</li><li>DG-FEM discretization</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-advec-diff.md) |
| `mp-heat-transfer` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>transient heat transfer</li><li>adiabatic, Dirichlet, radiation and free convection boundary conditions</li><li>CG-FEM discretization</li><li>diffuse-interface and CutFEM-based two-phase operators based on a prescribed interface description</li><li>optional: prescribed velocity fields</li><li>optional: laser heat source modeling</li><li>optional: solidification</li><li>optional: evaporative cooling</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-heat-transfer.md) |
| `mp-level-set` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>level-set transport and reinitialization</li><li>prescribed velocity fields</li><li>inflow, outflow, inflow-outflow, and wetting boundary conditions</li><li>evaporation-induced interface motion</li><li>curvature and normal-vector evaluation</li><li>CG-FEM and DG-FEM discretizations</li><li>hyperbolic, elliptic and geometric reinitialization methods</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-level-set.md) |
| `mp-melt-pool` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>incompressible two-phase flow with diffuse-interface description (CG-FEM)</li><li>Diffuse- (CG-FEM) or sharp-interface (CutFEM) heat transfer</li><li>Level-set based interface capturing</li><li>Evaporation with unreolved or resolved vapor flow</li><li>Temperature-dependent surface tension</li><li>Solidification modeling</li><li>Laser heat source</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-melt-pool.md) |
| `mp-radiative-transport` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>radiative transport equation</li><li>two-phase domains with prescribed interface geometries</li><li>intensity field computation</li><li>diffuse surface heat source computation</li><li>laser energy deposition modeling</li><li>CG-FEM discretization</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-radiative-transport.md) |
| `mp-reinit` | <div style="width: 25em; white-space: normal;"><ul><li>level-set reinitialization</li><li>homogeneous Neumann, Dirichlet, and wetting boundary conditions</li><li>signed-distance/tanh-based level-set fields</li><li>Hyperbolic, elliptic, and geometric reinitialization methods</li><li>CG-FEM and DG-FEM discretizations</li></ul></div> | [parameters](mp-reinit.md) |
