Detailed Description

template<int dim, typename number>
class MeltPoolDG::PostProcessingTools::DivergenceCalculator< dim, number >

Compute the integral of the divergence over the domain Ω of a vector, e.g. for the velocity u

∫ ∇·u dΩ Ω
